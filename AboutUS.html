About Us

Our Creators

Our team consists of a group of passionate students who think beyond the concept of the hypotetical box. Chosing to share and create with the basis of making eduction a creative venture rather than a cognitive one.

Game Description

Players must use their wits and instincts to survive, exploring vibrant underwater ecosystems, along the way, learning about the importance of turtle conservation.The game combines educational elements with immersive gameplay, teaching players about the importance of conservation and how human actions impact marine life.

Mission

To create a system where education is based on one's ability to think and not how well they can memorize. To break the barrier of innovation and remind young people that there is a world that needs our help to heal.
